About Us

As the Mobile Car Cleaning Experts, Nanotek Middle East specialises in cutting-edge technology using an Advanced Liquid Polymer Nanotechnology that promises to leave your car with an unparalleled, showroom finish. When our exclusive technology is applied to your vehicle, it encapsulates all the surface dirt and acts as a lubricant between the dirt and the paint, ensuring all dirt is removed without any scratching. The Nanotek Australia treatments leave your vehicle with a protective polymer coating that has been engineered specifically to give your car a superior polished finish, protect your car for up to 6 weeks and minimise the appearance of existing scratches!

Our team of Mobile Car Cleaning Experts are operational throughout Australia. On booking, we will come and meet you anywhere; whether it’s your home, your office car park, on the street or even if your car is still in the showroom!

ounded in Australia in 2004, under the original brand name Ecowash Mobile, by current CEO Jim Cornish, Nanotek is now a global phenomenon with an ever growing reach extending to New Zealand, The Middle East and Eastern and Western Europe. Nanotek is a multi-award winning franchise system and considered an industry leader and innovator in the car cleaning and detailing sector. Nanotek’s service is backed by its own outstanding product range comprising of over twenty exclusive hi-tech car care products.

We are currently experiencing a period of strong growth both domestically and internationally and whether as a valued client, or as part of the Nanotek Australia team, we look forward to you being a part of our future.
